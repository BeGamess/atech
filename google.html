How to use Google

Hello, in this article we have it about using Google.

How to make documents

To make documents, type "docs.google.com" in the searchbar and then press enter. You will get a menu popping up. Just press on the X button to close it and press on the + icon to make a new document.

How to make presentations

To make presentations, type "slides.google.com" in the searchbar and then press enter. You will get a menu popping up. Just press on the X button to close it and press on the + icon to make a new presentation, it's a little bit the same as documents.

How to make spreadsheets

To make spreadsheets, type "docs.google.com/spreadsheets" in the searchbar and then press enter. You will get a menu popping up. Just press on the X button to close it and press on the + icon to make a new spreadsheet, it's a little bit the same as presentations.

How to make forms

To make forms, type "docs.google.com/forms" in the searchbar and then press enter. You will get a menu popping up. Just press on the X button to close it and press on the + icon to make a new form, it's a little bit the same as spreadsheets.

How to acces everything

To get acces to everything on different platforms, login to your Google account and then open up the program you want and you get your stuff back!

How to share files

To share files, type "drive.google.com" in the searchbar and then press enter. Then you can press new and select a file or drag and drop one. Wait until its done. Now it's on your drive. To share it press the people icon with a + icon and copy the link. You can now put that link someone for other people to download!
